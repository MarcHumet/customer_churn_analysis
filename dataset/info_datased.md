

This dataset contains customer records used to analyze and predict churn behavior. Each entry includes features such as customer demographics, account information, service usage patterns, and a churn indicator.

The goal is to identify factors that contribute to customer churn and develop models that can predict which customers are likely to leave. This dataset is ideal for classification tasks, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and business intelligence dashboards.

Use cases include:

Building churn prediction models using machine learning

Analyzing trends and patterns that lead to customer loss

Informing customer retention strategies

All personal identifiers have been removed to maintain privacy.
